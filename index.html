How to Use in PowerPoint

Follow these simple steps to edit your converted diagram in PowerPoint

1. Download Your SVG

After conversion, click the download button to save the SVG file to your computer

2. Insert in PowerPoint

In PowerPoint, go to Insert > Pictures > This Device and select your downloaded SVG file

3. Convert to Shapes

Right-click on the image and select "Convert to Shape" from the context menu

4. Ungroup Elements

Right-click again and choose "Ungroup" to edit individual elements

5. Edit Your Diagram

Modify colors, resize elements, and edit text directly in PowerPoint

Pro Tips:

  • After ungrouping, you might need to ungroup a second time to access all elements
  • Use PowerPoint's "Selection Pane" (under Home > Select) to manage complex diagrams
  • Change colors using PowerPoint's "Format Shape" options
  • For best results, avoid resizing before converting to shapes

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pic2Chart

How does Pic2Chart work?
Pic2Chart uses advanced image tracing algorithms to convert your PNG or JPG diagrams into SVG format. The SVG files are optimized for PowerPoint, allowing you to convert them to editable shapes with a simple right-click.
Are my files secure?
Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are not stored on any servers. We prioritize your privacy and data security.
What types of diagrams work best?
Pic2Chart works best with clear, high-contrast diagrams such as flowcharts, organizational charts, process diagrams, and technical schematics. For best results, use images with minimal noise and clear shapes.
Can I edit text after conversion?
After converting the SVG to shapes in PowerPoint, text elements become editable text boxes. You can modify the content, font, size, and color just like any other text in PowerPoint.
What are the file size limits?
Pic2Chart supports files up to 10MB. For best performance, we recommend using images with dimensions between 500-2000 pixels per side.
